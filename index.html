Our Services

We developed web-based systems that provide us with real-time, reliable, and organized data making our operations fast and efficient

Point-Of-Sale (POS) System

Records sales transactions and updates all relevant accounts like sales, inventory, and the like real time making just in time restocking possible. Also capable of reservations and subscription sales

Warehouse/Inventory Systems

Tracks all inventory movement real time producing updated inventory count. Records accountable person for control on inventory and lessening the risk of theft.

Accounting Systems

Monitors cash, sales, expenses, receivables, loans, and liquidation movements giving an overall view of the company’s standing. Can also provide more accurate specific reports and data comparison.

HR/Payroll Systems

Records, monitors and reports employee performance and needs. Automates payroll, deductions, and loan application lessening effort and possible inaccuracies.

Company Websites

Ranging from simple, straight to the point sites to sites with product selling features, we make sites that is functional and reflects your corporate identity.

With Apollo Systems, we get…

Real-time information

You get all the information that you need real-time allowing you to closely monitor your business and to make timely decisions such as replenishing inventory, delivering goods to customers, andpaying due expenses, averting potential problems like stock shortage, customer complaints, and payable penalties. The cross-platform feature of Apollo Systems means you have access to everything, anytime, anywhere.

Integrated and Manageable Data

All the information that is entered in the system is synched and integrated to create one big database where you can pull out specific information without a problem. Need a comparison of sales per branches? How about sales per item per branch? Who’s your top performing employee? These are just a few clicks away.

Efficiency

Time wasted is money lost. Streamlining your operations removes redundant procedures that take up precious business hours freeing you and your employees up so you can focus on other aspects of the business.

Less room for human error

Traditional procedures may require several people to work on the data increasing the probability of making mistakes along the ways. These extra procedures are automatically done by the system giving more accurate and reliable information.
